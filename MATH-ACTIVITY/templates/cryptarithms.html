Cryptarithms

Cryptarithm kya hota hai?

Cryptarithm ek maths puzzle hota hai jisme numbers ki jagah letters likhe hote hain. Hame har letter ke liye ek unique digit find karna hota hai taaki equation sahi ho jaaye.

Cryptarithm ek puzzle hai jisme letters numbers ko represent karte hain aur hume logic use karke un letters ke sahi digits dhoondhne hote hain.

Puzzle

  TWO
+ TWO
------
 FOUR

Is puzzle me har letter ek alag digit ko represent karta hai. Same letter ka digit same hota hai aur koi bhi number 0 se start nahi hota.

Solution

  • T = 7
  • W = 3
  • O = 4
  • F = 1
  • U = 6
  • R = 8

Verification

TWO  = 734
TWO  = 734
-----------
FOUR = 1468

734 + 734 = 1468

Conclusion: This cryptarithm is solved correctly using logical thinking.
