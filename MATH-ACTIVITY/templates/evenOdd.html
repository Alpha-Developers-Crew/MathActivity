Even & Odd Numbers

What are even and odd numbers?

Simply ye dekho, ki any number which is divisible by 2 is even any number which is not divisible by 2 is odd

examples => 78, 66, 2, 4, 6, 8, 0 Ye sab even numbers hai

examples => 11, 55, 635, 85, 3, 7 Ye sab odd numbers hai

In sab se mujhe kya fayda?

Jab hame kisi bhi chiz ko 2 equal sections me divide karna rehta hai tab ham dekhte hai ki wo chiz even hai ya odd. Agar odd hai to equally divide nhi ho payega 2 sections lekin agar even rha to ho jayega. Ise parity bhi kaha jata hai
