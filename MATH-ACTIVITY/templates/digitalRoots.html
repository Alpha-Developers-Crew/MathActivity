Digital Roots

Ye digital root hai kya

Chahe number kitna bhi bada ho, digit sum karte-karte aakhir me 1 se 9 ke beech ek number hi bachta hai.

Why do we study Digital Root?

  • Number patterns samajhne ke liye
  • Fast checking (addition, divisibility)
  • Logical thinking develop karne ke liye
  • Maths ko fun banane ke liye

Example

Suppose number = 458
4 + 5 + 8 = 17
1 + 7 = 8

Digital Root of 458 = 8

Observation

Ki kisi bhi 9 se divisible number ka digital root 9 hi hoga

Agar digital root 9 nhi hai to jo digital root hai wo remainder hoga 9 se us number ko divide karne ke baad
